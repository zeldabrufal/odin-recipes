Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 2 or 3 slices bread (depending on hunger)
  • Something to put on bread, ideas below (optional)
    • Butter
    • Peanut Butter
    • Jam
    • Marmite
    • Marmalade
    • Garlic and Olive oil (Spanish)
      1. Put bread in toaster and toast
      2. Take bread (now toast) out of toaster
      3. Add whatever you want to the toast
      4. Eat immediately
