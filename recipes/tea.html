English Breakfast Tea

Ingredients

  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Milk (splash)
  • Sugar (Optional)
  1. Boil Kettle
  2. Pour boiled water into mug with the tea bag in it
  3. Leave for at least 60 seconds
  4. Stir and poke tea bag with spoon, then remove tea bag
  5. Add a splash of milk
  6. Add sugar if you like
