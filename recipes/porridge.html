Porridge

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup to 1 cup Oats (depending on hunger)
  • Water (twice volmume of oats)
  • Spoon of Tahini
  • Raspberries (Fresh or Frozen, however many you like)
  • Sugar/Honey/Maple Syrup (Optional)
  1. Add oats to pan and heat on stove on a medium heat until boiling
  2. Turn the heat down and stir with a spoon until the desired consistency
  3. Add raspberries and stir
  4. Transfer to bowl of choice and drizzle Tahini on top
